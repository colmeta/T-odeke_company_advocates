Our Story

Founded in 2004 by Thomas Odeke, T Odeke & Co. Advocates began with a simple mission: to provide world-class legal representation to Ugandan individuals and businesses.

Over two decades, we've grown from a single office in Nakasero to three strategic locations across Uganda, serving hundreds of clients and winning countless cases.

Today, we're recognized as one of Uganda's leading law firms, trusted by multinational corporations, government entities, and individuals alike.

Our Milestones

  • 2004 Firm founded in Nakasero, Kampala
  • 2010 Nsambya office opened
  • 2015 500th case successfully resolved
  • 2020 Soroti office serving Eastern Uganda
  • 2026 Recognized as Uganda's Premier Legal Authority
